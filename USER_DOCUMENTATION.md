## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

#### Step 1: Visit the Platform
- Go to https://mkulimalink.vercel.app
- Click "Sign Up" button

#### Step 2: Enter Basic Information
- **Full Name**: Your complete name
- **Email**: Valid email address
- **Phone**: Your phone number (format: +255XXXXXXXXX)

#### Step 3: Set Password
- **Password**: Minimum 6 characters
- **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password

#### Step 4: Select Your Role
- **Farmer**: If you grow and sell agricultural products
- **Buyer**: If you purchase agricultural products
- **Supplier**: If you supply seeds, fertilizers, or equipment

#### Step 5: Complete Your Profile
- **Region**: Select your location region
- **District**: Select your district
- **Farm Size** (Farmers only): Enter your farm size in acres
- **Business Name** (Buyers only): Enter your business name

### Logging In
1. Go to https://mkulimalink.vercel.app
2. Click "Sign In"
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click "Sign In" button

### Resetting Password
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your email
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Follow the link to create new password

## For Farmers

### Listing Products

#### Step 1: Navigate to List Product
1. Log in to your account
2. Click "Sell Products" or "List Product"
3. Fill in product details

#### Step 2: Product Information
- **Product Name**: e.g., "Organic Tomatoes"
- **Category**: Select from dropdown (Vegetables, Grains, Fruits, etc.)
- **Description**: Detailed product description
- **Quality**: Premium, Standard, or Economy
- **Organic**: Check if product is organic certified

#### Step 3: Pricing & Quantity
- **Price**: Price per unit in TZS
- **Unit**: kg, ton, bag, piece, liter, dozen, or bundle
- **Quantity**: Amount available for sale

#### Step 4: Location & Harvest
- **Region**: Your farming region
- **Harvest Date**: When the product was harvested

#### Step 5: Upload Images
- Click "Upload Images"
- Select up to 5 images (max 5MB each)
- Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, WebP

#### Step 6: Submit
- Review all information
- Click "List Product"
- Your product is now live!

### Managing Your Products

#### View Your Listings
1. Click "My Products" in menu
2. See all your active listings
3. View sales and inquiries

#### Edit Product
1. Click on product
2. Click "Edit"
3. Update information
4. Click "Save Changes"

#### Delete Product
1. Click on product
2. Click "Delete"
3. Confirm deletion

#### Track Sales
1. Go to "Sales Dashboard"
2. View:
   - Total revenue
   - Number of orders
   - Top selling products
   - Sales trends

### Best Practices for Sellers

#### Product Photos
- Use clear, well-lit photos
- Show product from multiple angles
- Include size reference (hand, coin, etc.)
- Avoid blurry or dark images

#### Product Description
- Be detailed and honest
- Mention quality and freshness
- Include harvest date
- Specify any certifications

#### Pricing
- Research market prices
- Consider quality level
- Factor in delivery costs
- Be competitive

#### Communication
- Respond to inquiries quickly
- Be professional and courteous
- Provide accurate information
- Honor your commitments

### Making a Purchase

#### Step 1: Add to Cart
1. View product details
2. Enter quantity
3. Click "Add to Cart"

#### Step 2: Checkout
1. Click "Cart" icon
2. Review items
3. Click "Checkout"

#### Step 3: Delivery Address
- Enter delivery region
- Enter delivery district
- Enter specific address
- Add delivery notes (optional)

#### Step 4: Select Payment Method
- **TigoPesa**: Pay via TigoPesa
- **HaloPesa**: Pay via HaloPesa
- **Airtel Money**: Pay via Airtel Money

#### Step 5: Complete Payment
1. Enter your phone number
2. You'll receive payment prompt on phone
3. Enter PIN to confirm
4. Payment processed

#### Step 6: Order Confirmation
- Order number displayed
- Estimated delivery date
- Seller contact information

## Payment Guide

### Supported Payment Methods

#### TigoPesa
- **Provider**: Tigo Tanzania
- **How to Use**:
  1. Have TigoPesa account
  2. Select TigoPesa at checkout
  3. Enter phone number
  4. Receive payment prompt
  5. Enter PIN to confirm

#### HaloPesa
- **Provider**: Halo Tanzania
- **How to Use**:
  1. Have HaloPesa account
  2. Select HaloPesa at checkout
  3. Enter phone number
  4. Receive payment prompt
  5. Enter PIN to confirm

#### Airtel Money
- **Provider**: Airtel Tanzania
- **How to Use**:
  1. Have Airtel Money account
  2. Select Airtel Money at checkout
  3. Enter phone number
  4. Receive payment prompt
  5. Enter PIN to confirm

### Payment Security

#### Your Information is Safe
- All payments encrypted
- PCI DSS compliant
- No credit card storage
- Secure servers

#### Transaction Confirmation
- Receive SMS confirmation
- See transaction ID
- Check order status online
- Get receipt via email

### Refunds

#### Requesting a Refund
1. Go to "My Orders"
2. Click on order
3. Click "Request Refund"
4. Select reason
5. Submit request

#### Refund Processing
- Seller reviews request
- Refund approved/denied
- If approved: refund within 3-5 days
- Notification sent via SMS

#### Refund Status
1. Go to "My Orders"
2. Click on order
3. See refund status
4. Track refund progress

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is MkulimaLink free to use?**
A: Yes, creating an account is free. Sellers pay a small commission on sales.

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: Email support@mkulimalink.com or use the in-app chat.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures.

### For Farmers

**Q: How long does it take for my product to be listed?**
A: Products are listed immediately after submission.

**Q: Can I edit my product after listing?**
A: Yes, you can edit price, quantity, and description anytime.

**Q: How do I get paid?**
A: Payments are transferred to your account after order delivery.

**Q: What commission do you charge?**
A: We charge 5% commission on successful sales.

### For Buyers

**Q: How long does delivery take?**
A: Delivery time depends on location, typically 2-5 days.

**Q: Can I cancel an order?**
A: Yes, if the seller hasn't confirmed yet.

**Q: What if the product is damaged?**
A: Contact the seller immediately with photos. We'll help resolve.

**Q: Is there a minimum order amount?**
A: No, you can order any quantity available.

### Payments

**Q: Is my payment information safe?**
A: Yes, we use encrypted payment processing.

**Q: Can I use multiple payment methods?**
A: Yes, you can choose any supported method.

**Q: What if payment fails?**
A: Try again or contact support.

**Q: How long does refund take?**
A: Refunds process within 3-5 business days.

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In
- Check email and password
- Verify caps lock is off
- Try password reset
- Clear browser cache
- Contact support

### Product Not Showing
- Check product status (active/inactive)
- Verify product details are complete
- Wait 5 minutes for update
- Refresh page
- Contact support

### Payment Issues
- Verify phone number format
- Check account balance
- Try different payment method
- Restart phone
- Contact support

### Order Not Delivered
- Check order status
- Contact seller
- Provide tracking information
- Request refund if needed
- Contact support
